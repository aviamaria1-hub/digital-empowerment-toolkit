Simple explanations for the whole family — from students to grandparents. No tech background needed.

📱

What is Technology?

Technology is any tool or "helper" created by people to solve a problem or make a job easier. It isn't just computers — even a wheel or a pencil is technology!

Real-World Example: A washing machine. Instead of scrubbing clothes by hand for hours, we use technology to do it faster.
🤖

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is a type of computer program that can "think," "learn," and "act" like a human. It's like giving a computer a brain so it can recognise pictures, solve puzzles, or talk back to you.

Real-World Example: When YouTube or Netflix suggests a movie they think you will like — that is AI learning your tastes!

Your Host & Curator

Meet Avia Maria Lindie

CEO AI Innovator Keynote Speaker Board Director

Avia Maria Lindie is a daughter of the Ancient County, born and raised in New Amsterdam, Berbice. She built and led businesses — including Jim Bacchus Travel and the Metro brand — without a degree, proving that courage and vision outrank credentials. A mother of four and a survivor of domestic violence, she transformed her deepest challenges into purpose.

When the pandemic reshaped the world in 2020, Avia launched Evolve Business Solutions Inc. and Collection by AVIA MARIA. She went on to co-create Avi-AI and Silvie AI — among the first AI tools built by a Guyanese woman, for Guyanese people and the Caribbean. She holds two Master's degrees, earned on her own terms, serves on the Board of the Caribbean Corporate Governance Institute, and is a proud Past President of the Rotary Club of Georgetown Central.

Her mission is simple: no woman in the Cinderella County — or anywhere — should be left behind by the digital future.
